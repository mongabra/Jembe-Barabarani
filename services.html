Our Services

We offer specialized transport services for farm produce, tailored to volume, distance and handling requirements.

🚛

Lorries (Large Trucks)

High capacity for bulk grain, packaged produce and pallets. Long-haul capable, with secure tarpaulin covers and loading support.

  • Capacity: up to 20 tonnes
  • Suitable for: maize, beans, packaged produce
  • Route: long and short distances
🚚

Pickups & Vans

Flexible and faster for smaller loads or urgent deliveries. Ideal for last-mile pickups from farms to collection centres.

  • Capacity: ~1–2 tonnes
  • Suitable for: vegetables, small crates
  • Route: short to medium distances
🐄

Animal Transport

Specialized trailers and trained handlers for live domestic animals to ensure humane and safe transport to processing facilities.

  • Special cages and ventilation
  • Handlers trained in animal welfare
  • Route planning for minimal stress
